South Africa's HIV Prevalence by District

In 2015, President's Emergency Plan for AIDS Relief (PEPFAR) shifted its program focus from supporting HIV treatment services in all 52 districts to achieving 90-90-90 in 27 priority districts, based on the greatest burden of PLHIV. District data is the most recent available (2012).
