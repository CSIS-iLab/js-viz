South Africa's HIV Prevalence by Province (15 - 49 Age Group)

This map shows HIV prevalence by province in South Africa in the 15 to 49 age group.
Click the play button to see the changes in HIV prevalence from 2002 to 2016. Click a year on the timeline to see the prevalence distribution. Hover over the map to see the HIV prevalence rate of a province in the selected year.

Sources:
Shisana O, Rehle T, Simbayi LC, Zuma K, Jooste S, Zungu N, Labadarios D,Onoya D et al. (2014)
South African National HIV Prevalence, Incidence and Behaviour Survey,2012. Cape Town: HSRC PressICF, 2012.
